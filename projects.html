Creative Eye construction and interiors specialises in interior design and construction of bare shell corporate and commercial spaces.

We assist our clients either with full turnkey design and build solutions, stand-alone interior design (space and theme planning), or project execution (contract construction) only.


Our services are tailored to the specific requirements of the individual client company, in each and every project.


Our team comes from a broad range of backgrounds, which enables us to operate effectively across our various service fields.


Amongst them, our team members have decades of experience with millions of square feet of space designed and built.


This knowledge and experience comes to fore to produce functional, sustainable and efficient environments for our clients, on time, and within budget.
